Entertain Yourself and Others

Here are two categories of songs. Obviously one is a collection of my favorite songs to play. The other is a category I like to call "Campfire Songs." This is for those times where there is a guitar, and people want to hear you play. They serve as real people pleasers, and provide an easy way to have a good time. I hope you enjoy.

Campfire Songs

  • Wonderwall by Oasis
  • (Good Riddance) Time of Your Life by Green Day
  • Free Falling by Tom Petty
  • Sweet Caroline by Neil Diamond
  • Wagon Wheel by Darius Rucker
  • I'm a Believer by The Monkees
  • Fast Car by Tracy Chapman
  • Photograph by Ed Sheeran
